Call Notes
Cerebrum is commercializing the first tire-mounted sensor technology that provides advanced insights beyond pressure monitoring - including tread depth (accurate to 1mm vs TPMS’s 3-4mm), rubber temperature for blowout prediction, and load monitoring. Founded as a 2019 spinoff by tire industry veteran Keith Ferry (16 years experience), the company targets the archaic $6B TPMS market with a $30B smart tire opportunity driven by regulatory tailwinds (Europe now mandates trailer pressure monitoring, DOT pressuring EV companies on wear monitoring). Their hardware-enabled SaaS model serves commercial fleets with proven ROI - 85% of downtime incidents were predicted before occurring through temperature alerts. With 30,000 sensors deployed generating $1.4M revenue (doubling annually) and first OEM partnership launching with Rivian in 2027, Cerebrum positions itself as the inevitable TPMS replacement.

Funding requirements:
-Currently raising $5-7M
-Seeking $25M pre-money valuation (continuing from 2023 round)
Decision + Reason
Further diligence required.

There's a real business here ($1.4M in ARR) yet the value-add for customers on top of traditional TPMS sensors needs to be validated. Also, the growth rate is a little slow to feel confident on VC scale returns. Industry partnerships could accelerate this company's trajectory.
